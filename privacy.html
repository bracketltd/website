Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This policy applies to every app published by Bracket Ltd. If an app's App Store privacy label reads "Data Not Collected," we collect nothing from it and nothing below applies.

What we collect and why

Apps collect only what their features require: account details, app content, purchase status, device information, and crash or usage data. We use it to run and improve our apps, restore purchases, answer support requests, and keep services secure. Some apps keep everything on your device and send us nothing.

Who else sees it

Apps with accounts or cloud features use Sign in with Apple and Cloudflare. Apps with purchases use Apple and RevenueCat. Apple handles payments; we never receive payment-card details. These providers are required to protect your data at least as well as we do. We do not sell your data or share it for advertising.

Retention and your choices

We keep data only as long as those purposes require. Where an app has accounts, you can delete yours in the app. You can also email support@bracket.ltd to access or delete anything we hold, or to withdraw consent.

Changes

We may update this policy. The current version will always be posted here.
