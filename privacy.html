Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This policy applies to every app published by Bracket Ltd.

Some apps collect nothing at all. Where an app's App Store privacy label reads "Data Not Collected," that app makes no network requests and the sections below do not apply to it.

Data

Some apps keep data only on your device. Others use the information needed for their features, including account details, app content, purchase status, device information, and crash or usage information.

Apps with accounts or cloud features may use Sign in with Apple and Cloudflare. Apps with purchases may use Apple and RevenueCat. Apple handles payments; we do not receive payment-card details.

Use and deletion

We use data to provide and improve our apps, restore purchases, provide support, and keep services secure. We keep cloud data only as long as needed. To ask about or delete data we hold, email support@bracket.ltd.

Changes

We may update this policy. The current version will always be posted here.
