About kingbit

kingbit is a modern, turn-based multiplayer game built entirely in SwiftUI. Challenge your friends to a game of strategy and wit, where every move counts.

Features

  • Online Multiplayer: Challenge friends or random opponents
  • Modern Design: Clean, minimal interface built with SwiftUI
  • Strategic Gameplay: Easy to learn but difficult to master
  • Multiple Game Modes: Classic, Blitz, and Custom variations
  • Statistics: Track your stats and climb the leaderboard

kingbit was designed to showcase the capabilities of SwiftUI while providing an engaging and fun gaming experience. The game combines classic turn-based strategy with modern design sensibilities.
